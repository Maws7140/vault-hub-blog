I’ve seen that when people make websites like these, they tend to shove a blog in it. I guess it kind of fits; I can be awfully opinionated and I needed the extra tab for visual balance, so here we are!

Hi, I’m gonna go by Maws on here. I’m a data science student diversifying and building my portfolio as the real world rapidly approaches me with everything that comes with it. I made the Storyteller Suite plugin when I started using Obsidian so I could write stories and keep them long-term. I ended up realizing this would be really cracked for school, and now improving my Obsidian workflow is unfortunately my primary hobby.

I'll mostly be writing about Obsidian-related stuff, or at least in that vein. If there's something big happening that I feel would be really nice to know as an Obsidian user, I'll probably write about that too.
